Zhi Tian

I am an algorithm expert in Meituan Inc.. Before that, I obtained a PhD in May 2021 at School of Computer Science, The University of Adelaide, Australia, advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen. I was also fortunate to work with Dr. Weilin Huang and Prof. Yu Qiao at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences.

I published a few well-known computer vision algorithms including the object detector FCOS, and the scene text detection method CTPN.
I was awarded Google Phd Fellowship in 2019.

We are always looking for talented interns/full-time researchers with strong coding skills and research experience. Please drop me an email if interested.

Links to: [GitHub] [Google Scholar]

Research

I am working to develop high-performance and practical algorithms for the following computer vision research problems:

  • Object detection, instance segmentation and semantic segmentation.
  • Scene text understanding (i.e., OCR).
